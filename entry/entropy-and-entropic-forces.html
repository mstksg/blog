Entropy and Entropic Forces

by Justin Le

Source ♦ Markdown ♦ LaTeX ♦ Posted in Computation, Physics ♦ Comments

Contents

There’s actually a bit of an air of mystery and misunderstanding around the idea of entropy. Notoriously misrepresented in popular science as the concept of “disorder”, the vague ideas associated with entropy have been used to argue things ranging from the origin of life to the inevitability of global self- destruction.
